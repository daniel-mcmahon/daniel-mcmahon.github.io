About

I'm Daniel

As a Data Analyst, I specialise in leveraging data analysis and data science techniques to enhance data-driven decisions and optimise organisational effectiveness. I create and maintain ETL frameworks, utilising Alteryx and Microsoft SQL Server Management Studio. Additionally, I provide BI reporting and dashboard creation, harnessing the power of Power BI, Tableau and Qlik. My skills include improving and maintaining data quality, providing insights to support business changes and developments, and delivering ad hoc reporting for all business requirements. I graduated from Sheffield Hallam University with a BSc in Mathematics. I am skilled in SQL, Alteryx, Power BI, DAX, Tableau, Qlik Sense, and Microsoft Excel. I am passionate about data analysis and problem solving, and I am always eager to learn new skills, languages and tools. I am certified in Alteryx Designer Core and Advanced, and I have completed various courses to improve my effeciency in SQL and Python. I am also skilled on PHP, HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
